## ISO/IEC 42001:2023 AI Management System (AIMS)
This repository serves as a technical knowledge base and engineering toolkit for implementing, maintaining, and auditing an AI Management System (AIMS) based on the ISO/IEC 42001:2023 standard.


## What this repository is
This repository bridges the gap between GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance) theory and engineering practice. It translates the requirements of the world's first international AI management standard into:
Operational Controls: Tangible workflows for AI lifecycle management.
Audit-Ready Documentation: Templates and evidence-collection artifacts for certification.
Compliance as Code: Technical logic mapping and automation scripts to validate AI system performance against ISO standards.
Every module in this series is documented from first principles, specifically designed to help organizations transition from "responsible AI" rhetoric to auditable, third-party verified systems.

## Why ISO 42001 matters

Unlike voluntary risk frameworks, ISO 42001 is certifiable. This repository focuses on the operational rigor required to pass an accredited third-party audit. By treating governance as a software engineering problem, we:
Reduce Friction: Automate compliance evidence collection for faster audit readiness.
Increase Reliability: Ensure that AI systems consistently adhere to safety and performance standards.
Enhance Accountability: Provide a verifiable trail of evidence for every model deployment, from development to production.
